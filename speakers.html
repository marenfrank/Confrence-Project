James Mickens

Mickens is a Computer Science tenured professor at Harvard School of Engineering & Applied Sciences. His research includes client-side web security, web performance and analysis, and storage architecture for large-scale web services. He has been published 20+ times and loves sharing and discussing his work and new ideas.

Mike Monteiro

Monteiro is from San Francisco and is the cofounder and design director of Mule Design. He advocates for simple and clean site design. He is passionate about design and has given talks on politics in design, the importance of hiring designers, and how to ensure you’re paid.

Susan Kare

Kare is a graphic designer best known for work in interface elements. She contributed to both the interface and type elements of the first Apple macintosh in the 1980s. She has also worked with Microsoft, IBM, Pinterest, and Facebook.

Ethan Marcotte

Marcotte is a designer, writer, and speaker. His work emphasizes efficiency, accessibility, and patterns. He has worked with People Magazine and The Boston Globe, as well, he has written two books: Responsive Web Design and Responsive Design: Patterns and Principles.

Tim Brown

Brown is a British designer and chair of IDEO. He is passionate about the interdisciplinary, multi-faceted nature of design. He applies design to unconventional areas such as service design, system design and strategy, and across sectors such as education, health, government, technology, the social sector, and business.

Ethan Zuckerman

Zuckerman is a media scholar, blogger, and Internet activist. He is the director of the MIT Center for Civic Media and an associate professor for the Practice in Media Arts and Sciences at MIT. He also wrote the book Rewire: Digital Cosmopolitans in the Age of Connection, which won the Zócalo Book Prize.

Zoë Quinn

Quinn is a video game developer, programmer, writer, and artist. She is most known for her “choose-your-own path” game Depression Quest that came out in 2013 and the Gamergate controversy. She has also worked as a narrative design consultant for Loveshack Entertainment's game Framed, and is currently working on a game starring Greg Sestero.
