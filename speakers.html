James Mickens

Mickens is a Computer Science tenured professor at Harvard School of Engineering & Applied Sciences. His research includes client-side web security, web performance and analysis, and storage architecture for large-scale web services. He has been published 20+ times and loves sharing and discussing his work and new ideas.

Kare is a graphic designer best known for work in interface elements. She contributed to both the interface and type elements of the first Apple macintosh in the 1980s. She has also worked with Microsoft, IBM, Pinterest, and Facebook.

Ethan Marcotte

Marcotte is a designer, writer, and speaker. His work emphasizes efficiency, accessibility, and patterns. He has worked with People Magazine and The Boston Globe, as well, he has written two books: Responsive Web Design and Responsive Design: Patterns and Principles.
